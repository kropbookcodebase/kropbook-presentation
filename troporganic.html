03 · Farm Didi

Specially trained women,
transforming agriculture.

Farm Didi are specially trained women who collect soil samples from farms across India. They are not scientists — they are trusted community members who carry a QR-tagged bag to a farmer's field. They are the first link in the chain that ends with national-scale agricultural intelligence.
Farm Didi Network · TropOrganic Ground Operations
👩‍🌾

Who they are

Specially trained women from local farming communities. Trusted, accessible, reliable.

📋

What they do

Soil sample collection with QR-tagged bags, QR-linked to farmer identity. Accurate, standardized, scalable.

🔗

Why it works

Trust cannot be automated. In rural India, a familiar face changes everything.
