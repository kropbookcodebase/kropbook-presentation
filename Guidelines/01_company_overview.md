## Business Description

Kropbook Agritech Pvt. Ltd. is engaged in integrated agricultural and farm-led activities revolving around cultivation, nurturing, and natural development of crops, fruits, and vegetables. Operations focus on improving soil health, maintaining farmlands, supporting crop growth, and post-harvest handling (grading, sorting, cleaning, packing) — all incidental to agriculture.
