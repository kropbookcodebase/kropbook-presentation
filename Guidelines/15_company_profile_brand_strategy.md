## Problem Statement — The Founding Story

In 2018, Kropbook onboarded Mr. Jadhav, a farmer in Lasalgaon, Nashik. Three diagnostic questions were asked: his last 3-year crop cycle, 3-year soil testing history, and last-used pesticide and fertilizer. This exercise revealed the structural gaps Kropbook was built to solve.

### Farmer Pain Points Identified

| Pain Point | Problem | Kropbook's Response |
|---|---|---|
| Costly Soil Testing | Rs. 300–400 per test, 5–6 day wait, often unreliable | Rapid, accurate, cost-effective portable soil testing device |
| Expensive Expert Advice | Local agronomists costly, limiting access | AI crop-specific recommendations at scale |
| Unaffordable Brand Products | Top-brand fertilizers/pesticides inaccessible | Formulation R&D for cost-efficient alternatives |
| Low Crop Yields | Inefficiencies from outdated farming methods | Data-driven approach to boost crop productivity |
| Information Gap | No access to tools for informed decision-making | Platform with insights, soil data, crop history |
| Market Access | Difficulty connecting with buyers and suppliers | Direct marketplace connecting farmers to buyers |
| Agritech Accessibility | Reliable agritech unaffordable or unreachable | Freemium mobile platform accessible to all |
| Limited Soil Testing Data | Soil health data unavailable | Patented device, cloud-synced reports |
| Delayed Recommendations | Guidance not timely | Real-time recommendations based on test + weather |
| Insufficient Knowledge Sharing | Siloed farming community | Community connecting farmers, agronomists, researchers |
| Inadequate Financial Services | Small farmers lack agri-credit | Collaboration with financial institutions for agri-loans |
| Resource Strain | Water, fertilizer, pesticide wastage | Sustainable farming optimization |
| Limited Income Potential | Low yield, poor resource efficiency | Data-driven decisions for income maximization |
| Backward Integration Gap | Farmers disconnected from agribusinesses | Connect to wholesalers, food processors directly |
| Forward Integration Gap | No access to export/supermarket channels | Direct connection to export markets |

## Technology Platform — Our Solution

### User-Centric Mobile App
Personalized hub for farmers to access profiles, monitor progress, participate in Kropbook Learning courses, buy and sell agricultural products. Caters to farmers, enterprises, financial institutions, and agricultural universities.

### Soil Health Analysis
Patented technology delivering detailed soil analysis reports with recommendations within 2–3 minutes per soil sample. Data instantly and automatically shared on cloud servers.

### Crop-Specific Recommendations
AI algorithms generate crop-specific guidance on planting, irrigation, and harvesting techniques.

### Data-Driven Insights
Actionable insights derived from big data analytics — helps farmers optimize operations and mitigate risks.

### Detailed Data Mapping
Maps all available data insights: soil health, farm area (acres/hectares), complete farmer profiles, soil quality, required solutions, and precise GPS coordinates of farms. Generates farm maps and maintains comprehensive crop history.

### Market Access
Connects farmers to markets, creating opportunities for better prices and improved profitability.

### Access to Financing
Through the platform, farmers access financial services including credit and insurance.

### Knowledge Sharing
Platform fosters knowledge sharing among the farming community, promoting best practices and adoption of modern techniques.

### Data Monetization
In-depth data analysis tracks exact requirements of specific areas, farmers, and even districts. Monetizable through services and API access.
