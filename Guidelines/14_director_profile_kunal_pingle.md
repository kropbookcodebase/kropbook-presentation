## Role at Kropbook

Kunal Pingle serves as Director and Chief Technology Officer. He leads the development of Kropbook's proprietary sensing and device ecosystem, engineered for robust and reliable performance in diverse and challenging agricultural conditions.

His mandate covers:
- Development and architecture of Kropbook's IoT-driven soil testing devices
- Farm automation technology and embedded hardware design
- Enterprise data systems and ERP integration across Kropbook's operations
- Hardware-software integration for the Kropbook Intelligent Platform
- Ensuring devices are engineered for rural deployment durability, scalability, and cost-efficiency

His cross-industry background (medical devices, EVs, consumer electronics) directly enables Kropbook's ability to deploy hardware in field conditions that are remote, resource-constrained, and ruggedized environments.
