## Key Observations

1. Revenue more than doubled in FY2024-25 — driven by commodity trading scale-up.
2. Gross margins improved significantly from ~6% (FY23) to ~37% (FY25) — reflecting better procurement cost control or product mix shift.
3. Trade receivables grew 3x in FY24 and 3x again in FY25 — credit risk concentration increasing.
4. Other expenses jumped 9x in FY25 (from Rs. 1.47 Cr to Rs. 13.17 Cr) — primarily R&D (Rs. 4.44 Cr) and freight (Rs. 4.97 Cr).
5. Cash position improved from near-zero in FY23 to Rs. 84.83 Lakhs in FY25.
6. Director unsecured loan of Rs. 16.16 Lakhs unchanged across 3 years.
7. No bank institutional debt — entirely self-funded from operations.
8. FY2025-26 annualized revenue at Rs. 75.49 Cr — company approaching 80-IAC turnover ceiling (Rs. 100 Cr).
