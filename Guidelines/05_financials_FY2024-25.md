## Corporate Information (from FY2025 Notes)

The Company is engaged in integrated agricultural and farm-led activities: cultivation, nurturing, and natural development of crops, fruits, and vegetables. Operations include soil assessment, land preparation, organic nourishment, irrigation support, crop care, harvesting, and post-harvest handling. The company does not engage in independent processing that changes the essential character of agricultural produce.
